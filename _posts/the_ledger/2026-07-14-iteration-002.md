*Week of June 14, 2026*

This week was mostly about continuing the foundations I started last week.

I spent time adding content to Soft Iterations, refining my social profiles, and slowly shaping the project into something that feels more aligned with where I’m headed.

Outside the blog, I continued working through my Exel course. My goals has been simple: one to two hours a day.

Nothing ambitious.

Just consistent.

One thing I’ve noticed is that smaller goals are much easier for me to maintain than larger ones. Knowing I only need to spend an hour or two on a task makes it easier to get started, and once I do, I usually keep going.

It’s also helping me build momentum. Instead of trying to learn everything at once, I’m building on yesterday’s progress one step at a time. 

I’m looking forward to getting beyond the fundamentals and into topics that challenge me a little more. My next goal is to start building projects that allow me to apply what I’m learning and create tangible examples of my work.

Nothing flashy this week.

Just another reminder that consistency tends to beat intensity in the long run.
