*Week of June 7, 2026*

Soft Iterations is still very much under construction, so this week was less about major breakthroughs and more about building foundations.

Most of my time was spent getting the site up and running, migrating older posts, and updating them to better reflect the direction I'm heading today. I also spent some time refreshing my social profiles and connecting with people whose work I want to learn from.

Nothing particularly groundbreaking happened this week, but that's okay.

This week was about laying foundations, building habits, and taking the first few steps forward.
We'll see where they lead.
