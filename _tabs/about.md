I started this blog to document my journey to finding my next career. I spent 10+ years as a project manager for a tech firm, and 2+ years as a software engineer. Turns out, I hate building apps for other people. And while deeply miss the leadership and delivery of large scale projects, I feel it in my heart that it's time to move on. What that looks like, I don't know, but this blog will stand as an archive of where I've been once I figure it out.

Until them, I'm a life-long learner, video game nerd, and book enthusiast. I will share my knowledge from past careers as well as new knowledge as I pursue my Bachelors in Business.

Here's to the future and lots of coffee!
