I started this blog to document my journey toward finding my next career.

I spent 10+ years as a project manager in tech and another 2+ years as a software engineer. Turns out, after spending a couple of years building apps for other people, I learned it isn't something I want to do for the next 20 years.

While I deeply miss the leadership, problem-solving, and delivery of large-scale projects, I feel in my heart that it's time for something new. What that looks like, I don't know yet, but this blog will stand as an archive of where I've been once I figure it out.

Until then, I'm a lifelong learner, video game nerd, and book enthusiast. I'll share lessons from my past careers, reflections on career transitions, and new knowledge as I pursue my Bachelor's degree in Business.

I don't have a master plan. I'm figuring things out one step at a time, and I suspect I'm not the only one.

Here's to the future, continuous learning, and lots of coffee.
